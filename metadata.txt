# Dataset Documentation

This document describes the datasets used in the Mental Health RAG Chatbot for retrieval-augmented generation.

---

## üìö Therapy Books

### 1. Cognitive Behavior Therapy: Basics and Beyond
- **Author**: Judith S. Beck, PhD
- **Purpose**: Core CBT theory and therapeutic conversation structure
- **Target Use**: Guides chatbot's cognitive restructuring approach
- **Key Topics**: Automatic thoughts, cognitive distortions, core beliefs, case conceptualization
- **Chunking**: By sub-chapter (1500 tokens, 200 overlap)

### 2. Mind Over Mood
- **Authors**: Dennis Greenberger, PhD & Christine A. Padesky, PhD
- **Purpose**: Practical patient-facing exercises and worksheets
- **Target Use**: Providing actionable techniques to users
- **Key Topics**: Thought records, activity scheduling, mood tracking, anxiety/depression worksheets
- **Chunking**: By exercise/worksheet (800 tokens, 100 overlap)

### 3. DBT Skills Training Manual
- **Author**: Marsha M. Linehan, PhD
- **Purpose**: Crisis management and emotion regulation protocols
- **Target Use**: Handling high-emotion situations and distress
- **Key Topics**: Distress tolerance, emotion regulation, interpersonal effectiveness, TIPP skills, mindfulness
- **Chunking**: By skill acronym (500 tokens, 50 overlap)

### 4. ACT Made Simple
- **Author**: Russ Harris, MD
- **Purpose**: Acceptance and Commitment Therapy concepts
- **Target Use**: Users who feel "stuck" or find CBT too rigid
- **Key Topics**: Psychological flexibility, defusion, acceptance, values clarification, the Hexaflex model
- **Chunking**: By metaphor/concept (1000 tokens, 150 overlap)

## üèõÔ∏è NIMH Articles

- **Source**: National Institute of Mental Health (nimh.nih.gov)
- **Count**: 173 articles
- **Type**: Clinical fact sheets and patient brochures
- **Purpose**: Authoritative factual information for medical questions
- **Key Fields**:
  - `Topic`: Broad category (e.g., "Anxiety Disorders")
  - `Title`: Article name
  - `Source_URL`: Link for citation
  - `Content`: Full article text
- **Usage**: Primary source for symptom definitions, treatment options, and clinical facts

---

## üí¨ Counseling Conversations

- **Source**: Hugging Face datasets
- **Count**: ~3,500 dialogue pairs
- **Type**: User-Therapist conversation exchanges
- **Purpose**: Few-shot learning for response style and tone
- **Key Fields**:
  - `Context`: User's statement or question
  - `Response`: Counselor's empathetic reply
- **Usage**: Teaching the model therapeutic communication patterns

---

## üîÑ Data Processing Pipeline

1. **PDF Extraction**: PyMuPDF extracts text from therapy books
2. **Chunking**: LangChain RecursiveCharacterTextSplitter with source-specific parameters
3. **Embedding**: HuggingFace Sentence Transformers (`all-MiniLM-L6-v2`)
4. **Storage**: ChromaDB vector databases (6 separate collections)
5. **Retrieval**: Semantic similarity search with intent-based routing
